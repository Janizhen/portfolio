3D-modeling (for this portfolio)

I made this portfolio website from scratch, including the background picture (the house, the clouds and the little cat)! The following is my process.

Process

Firstly, I expored some shapes of clouds, and tried the lighting for the model. Large area lights which placed close to the object can create subtle and refined lighting effect.

And then, I made some combination of the clouds, tried the lights and checked its reflection on the clouds, and also build a house in the model.

Finishing the details of the model, adding materials, background and lights, adjusting camera and rendering settings.

Render and complete!
